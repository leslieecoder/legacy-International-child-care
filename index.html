Our Mission

Legacy International offers educational services that guarantee the safety of your children by providing quality education, promoting the stages of development: cognitive, affective, social, language, sensory and motor development, which is stimulated through supervised play activities. Recognizing that children achieve their maximum potential in the context of relationships based on trust and respect, as we appreciate childhood as a unique and valuable stage in the life cycle of the human being.

Our Vision

Provide personalized attention working with small groups, to develop the learning areas of our students according to their age. We create a family atmosphere in our daycare with the objective of facilitating the adaptation process, to create a daycare chain in the cities surrounding Lehi. We guarantee safety under the care and supervision of trained personnel with English and Spanish training, maintaining fluid and easily accessible communication with our parents.

Quality & Care

Every child deserves the best start in life. Our experienced team is dedicated to providing a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where children can grow, learn, and thrive with personalized attention.

What We Offer

Comprehensive childcare services with bilingual education and personalized attention for your child's development.

Bilingual Education

English and Spanish instruction providing a strong foundation for global communication skills.

Montessori Methodology

Proven educational approach emphasizing independence, self-directed learning, and child-led discovery.

Small Group Attention

Personalized care in small groups ensuring each child receives individual attention and support.

Holistic Development

Development of linguistic, social, intellectual, physical, and emotional areas of learning.

Healthy Nutrition

Balanced, nutritious meals supporting healthy growth and development.

BrightWheel App
